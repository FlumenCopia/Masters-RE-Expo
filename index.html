🎯 GenZ Activation Strategy
Solar Is The Next Big Career Move.

Position the expo as "where your solar career begins." Tap into Kerala youth anxieties about jobs, entrepreneurship, and the future. Make it feel like a festival, not a trade fair.

🏆 Solar Startup Challenge
Instagram + LinkedIn contest. Best solar startup pitch wins a free stall + mentorship. 50+ entries = massive UGC.
🎓 Campus Ambassador Program
15 colleges × 2 ambassadors = 30 active content creators. Certificate of participation drives sign-ups.
🎶 Evening Entertainment (7–9 PM)
Live music + artist collab makes the event shareable. Youth attend for entertainment, stay for solar career opportunities.
📲 Influencer Collab Layer
5 Kerala micro-influencers (50K–500K followers) in tech, career, sustainability niches. Barter + ₹20–50K per creator.

Risk Register
Risks, Mitigation & Contingency

Every campaign at this scale has risks. Identifying them early prevents surprises. All mitigations are already built into the timeline.

Risk Likelihood Impact Mitigation
Low vendor stall registrations by Aug 1 Medium High WhatsApp direct outreach to 700+ member network Week 1. Incentive: early-bird stall discount for first 50 bookings.
Title sponsor not confirmed by Aug 15 Medium High Start sponsor outreach July 1. Prepare 3-tier contingency: one Title, two Co-sponsors to make up same revenue. Name confirmation public announcement creates more pressure.
Meta ad CPL too high (>₹500/lead) Medium Medium A/B test 3 creative variants per campaign. Reallocate budget from underperforming ad sets weekly. Lookalike audience from existing member list.
Competing event announcement (non-KREEPA) Low Medium Finalize venue lock-in confirmation early. Use Ministry association and 700+ member backing as moat. PR coverage establishes primacy.
Content production delays Medium Medium Batch produce 4 weeks ahead. Content bank of 20+ ready-to-post assets always maintained. 2-week buffer built into all schedules.
Low GenZ attendance Medium Low Campus ambassador program starts Month 1. Solar Startup Challenge incentivizes participation. Evening entertainment is the conversion hook.
Website / registration form downtime during campaign Low High Technical audit + uptime monitoring from Day 1. Backup WhatsApp registration path always communicated in ads.
